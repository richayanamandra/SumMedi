# 2025AB UMLS Metathesaurus Precomputed Subsets

Ready-to-use subsets of the UMLS Metathesaurus that require no installation or customization. As of 2024AB, precomputed subsets include obsolete and suppressible content. These subsets do not include MetamorphoSys, the Semantic Network, or the Specialist Lexicon and Lexical Tools.
UMLS Dataset: https://www.nlm.nih.gov/research/umls/licensedcontent/umlsknowledgesources.html
